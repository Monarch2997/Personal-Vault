Archetype of [[Galahad Ozzie Davenport]]

Based around getting benefits from vengeance. Absorbing the power of the betrayers to make Galahad richer and put him on top.

Focused around Aurum, a power currency. He starts combat with an okay set amount.

When enemies hurt him, they take away a small amount of aurum from him, while generating a lot. (ex. Galahad is shot, enemy takes 5 aurum from Galahad, and generates 7, now that enemy has 12 aurum). This aurum can provide bonuses to them depending on the amount stored(usually just a simple stat buff)

This aurum is "in debt" to Galahad. If he hurts enemies who have accumulated Aurum, he can take it back. This aurum can be used for powerful new abilities. 

This system of, you hurt me, I hurt you and become better, is not only more fitting to his character, but also allows for Galahad to play more of an aggressive commander and tank. He has to get in there and start taking hits for his allies in order to generate large scale aurum. If he cannot tank, its not an unusable archetype because of the base starting amount. 

Every ability should scale depending on aurum. If I wanna pull a crazy gambit and bank all my aurum on my 1st ability, I should be able to and it should do something interesting. I'm not saying to make every ability power balanced, but it shouldn't be awful to throw a lot of aurum into low rank abilities

Aurum can drop to zero if I spend it all. Enemies cannot take enough Aurum to zero, only to a minimum of RANK.
