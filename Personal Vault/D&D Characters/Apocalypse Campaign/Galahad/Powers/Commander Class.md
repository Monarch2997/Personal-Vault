### Rank 1

#### Combat Style

_Always Active_  
You may choose a [Combat Style](https://silencing-rules.forestry.md/404) to claim as your own.

#### Beacon of Hope

Aura power. Once per short rest, as an action(3 action points), supercharge aura for 1 minute. Multiple beacons of hope don't stack. Effects do not apply to you, unless allies are in aura.

- +1 to Checks and [Saving Throws](https://silencing-rules.forestry.md/appendix/skills/saving-throws/). Supercharge adds `Hope Catalyst` to the bonus.
- +1 AC. Supercharge adds `Hope Catalyst` to the bonus.
- 30ft Range. Supercharge adds `Hope Catalyst * 10`ft to the range.
- Supercharge only: Allies within range gain 1 point of inspiration. This point may be gained once per [Medium Rest](https://silencing-rules.forestry.md/appendix/survival-rules/rest/#medium-rest-3-hours).
- Enemies within this range take 50% more damage. Supercharge doubles this effect.
- Allies gain +1 to attack roles while enemies gain -1 to attack roles. Supercharge adds `Hope Catalyst to the bonus`.
