After plunging the human stranger into the mud, The outworlder looked around. They saw a land corrupted with life. Lush trees, green grass, and abundant bugs in the air were a horrid sight to them. They ran forward, tearing through the trees, struggling to run with how his body was reacting to the new reality. He felt deeply bounded and dense. Before he could spread himself out, but now he couldn't. He suddenly was barely able to change and miniplate his own matter as they could before. He could only move a few atoms deep within his own body. This new reality had dug its talons into him and would not let the Outworlder have the same freedom as before. He had no spells, no weapons, nothing to use as power. Without his immense self-control, he spun out wildly. he ripped and tore into the different plants around them, cursing them for infesting this reality as well. His rampage was without caution, and after ripping the branch from a tree, the weight was too much and he fell backward, tumbling down into a valley. 

The blazing sun awoke him. He lay in the crevice of large rocks, his body ravaged with pain. Near every bone in his body had been broken and his stomach lay split open. Yet they did not die, through immense fury and drive, he would not die surrounded by life. He quickly understood that traveling into this new reality forced him to take mortal form. Death was now real, he had organs and bones, and he would have to fight back to take control of his matter once again. They lay in that crevasse for hours attempting to reconnect with his suppressed matter manipulation abilities. 

After arduous pain and strife, they forced their bones to reconnect, their stomach to close, and body to return to its previous state. His arms regained their strength and ripped him out of the hole. He left with new understanding. True reality is an abomination, just as the lands trapped within the fabric of reality. He must escape all places, to free himself of life. He will regain his control and become as powerful as before. Then, he can unravel the truth of how to gain more souls and psychic might in order to tear a new hole through reality. Then, he may tear himself out and create a new land outside of reality, a beautiful dreamland without life.

The Outworlder imagined this wonderful place. Lands of melting, everchanging lava. Mountains of might standing over great valleys of colorful deserts. Oceans of boiling, twisting liquids. An atmosphere toxic for life with flowing storms of chaos and lightning. This dreamland would be called... **Tarcas**. And to remind themselves of their past land, their great failure, the Outworlder named himself, **Kalgorath**.

From there, Kalgorath set forth. He travelled the land seeking civilization. He eventually tracked down the weather station Eddie was based out of. To break in, he had to get through the locked door silently during the night. After arduous strain, he moved some of the atoms within his finger into the lock. He was able to open the lock from the inside. He then recollected himself and moved in. 

His footsteps were loud and heavy, due to Kalgorath struggling to handle his new uncontrolled density and weight
