### Kalgorath during the Kicharii Invasion
*This part will need the most redo work and planning as this is the session of Kalgorath during the Finale.*
Kalgorath will be mostly unaware of the Kicharii until too late. With all forces capable of fighting them trapped within their separate realms, there is no one to detect them as the enter. Structurally, the realms of Kalgorath go:

1. Heaven
2. Kalgorath 
3. Hell

And then the realm of magic orbits the three.

The first to see the Typhon will be Heaven. The threat will be analyzed by angelic scouts. Them and the Kicharii will engage in quick battle, with the angels tearing through Kicharii forces but being forced to retreat due to their number. Heaven will quickly recognize the Typhon as an otherworldly threat to their reality. **The Holy Maker MUST BE AWAKENED**

The angels will open his chambers, and see the Holy Maker trapped in his slumber. They will see a small note left in front of the Holy Maker's resting spirit.

	"IOU a wakeup call"

The angels would try everything to awake their sleeping god but fail in every attempt. No power, no force, no anything could wake him. To make matters worse, the Kicharii had begun to devastate Heaven.

Around half of heaven has already been consumed, with many souls and angels turned into Kicharii. The war is being lost, few angels remain. The Typhon has breached Heaven, and is burrowing down. It will reach Kalgorath, the prized realm. If they can convert 3/4 of the Holy Maker's matter into Kicharii, that matter is lost forever.

Without possible solution, the Angels turn towards the worse decision they have ever made, they broke their vows to the Holy Maker. They would interfere with the other realms, and open gateways between them, all connecting to heaven.

#### Hell:

##### Lore: 
Gates from hell powered legions of demons onto the Kicharii forces. They ripped and tore apart any living soul or creature in sight. They're numbers formed a blockade against the Kicharii advance, but they are no true match for the power of the Kicharii. 

One group from Hell, the Cult of the Wretched, snuck away from the warside. This group, previously led by one known as the Runemaker, had already been sneaking into Kalgorath to plant evil and destruction. The had disregarded the warnings of the Dawn of Reality story and spoken with the orb. Corrupted, they now seek to bring his will of life's end to all of reality.

The Cult of the Wretched made way for the Holy Maker's temple. There they have been attempting to sneak in, open a portal to hell, and bring the Holy Maker to the Orb. To force him to free the Orb, and to merge them as the Orb demanded.
##### Gameplay:
Most main battlefields will include hosts of demons that are foe to the party and Kicharii. Demon portals are often opened by Angels to stop advances of Kicharii.

The party can choose to fight for or against the Cult of the Wretched, depending on how much they agree or disagree with the philosophy of the Orb. The Kicharii are the greatest proof of why life is horrid.

#### The Realm of Magic
##### Lore: 
Magic pours forth into Heaven like an endless tide. Fountains of energy crash into Kalgorath and Kicharii forces alike, obliterating all in its path. The flow of pure magic and divine power have been slowly adapted into the Kicharii, but with the magic coming in such a high concentration and power, it has been a challenge to integrate. Hardest of all is the Black Wind, a force of pure magical might. This wind ravages across all lifeforms, turning them into monsters without control.

After waves of magic end, Angels have looked into the realm of Magic. Unfortunately, the truth has been revealed, even through the chaos of magic, the realm is as wonderful and natural as it was left. The Orb was at least correct about how if you take life out of land, the land remains pure. No matter what natural or magical phenome happen, the beauty of a lifeless world cannot be destroyed.
##### Gameplay:
All across Heaven, insane magical effects pour out. Feel free to throw any sort of arcane chaos into the mix. All doors are unlocked, all options available. 
#### Kalgorath

##### Lore: 
Portals to Heaven are opened across Kalgorath. All over the land, Angels emerge. They ask of the people to help fight the Kicharii, to save their realm. Just like their Holy Maker, they are naïve. Life across Kalgorath erupts in chaos. Religious idols are torn down, now being proven false. War and conflict break out across the land, as now is the last chance for any to accomplish their great goals. Life across Kalgorath, seeing the end times near, delves into Insanity and destruction. Lands are burnt, thousands are killed, and nations crumble.

Many do choose to fight for their realm, holding great morals within themselves. The party is one such group asked to fight in this world.
##### Gameplay:
Forces from across Kalgorath are often led through these portals, agreeing to fight against the end of their reality. These act as support units.


**THE MISSION OF THE PARTY**
For the party, the player characters of the finale will have to hunt down the party as the portals to heaven are opening and the land is erupting into chaos. 

They will eventually learn that the party they are seeking has entered Heaven, seeking to save their world. They will find them on the battlefields, fighting Kicharii while torrents of magic blast around them and demons rage at their sides. Angels soar the skies cleaving through hordes.

When they attempt to enter heaven through a portal, an angel stops them and immediately recognizes that they are not from this world. They are not made of the same matter that the Holy Maker held. The angel, Calphus, will demand answers. The biggest one being, *"The Kicharii are not from our world, and neither are you, but you are not conquerors like them. Truthfully you are more like us creations of Kalgorath. What and who are you?"* The angel will attempt to get them a deal, where he will assist in them finding the party they seek, but they will come to the temple of the Holy Maker and use whatever outworlder knowledge they have to awaken him. With the Holy Maker's power, they can destroy the Kicharii. On the battlefields, they also see the work of the Cult of the Wretched. The Kalgorath party recognizes them as being similar to the Rune maker. They are not helping the fight, but rather draining power and life from all sides, then retreated back into portals.

The group will then make their way inside the temple, learns its stories and lore, and finally see a great green light emanating from the Holy Maker's chamber. They only have so much time, the light grows greener. Immense magical locks have been placed on it(puzzles and shit for the party). After finding a way past the door, they see in the chamber, the Cult of the Wretched is working to corrupt the Holy Maker. They are performing two rituals. One to open a portal to the lowest depths of hell, and the other to force the Holy Maker awake through sacrifice and stolen power, through the power of the orb.

If the portal ritual is successful, then evil magics pour into the temple, corrupting it.

If the awakening ritual is successful, then the Holy Maker is awakened with markings of the Orb imprinted on his spirit. 

The party must try to stop both, if they are successful, they can then awaken the Holy Maker on their own. They can do so through (RITUAL I WILL FIGURE OUT)

Over the course of the session, these are the party missions:
	1. Find the Kalgorath party and recruit them
	2. Find the party in heaven
	3. Awaken the Holy Maker 
	4. Stop the Rituals
	5. Flee
